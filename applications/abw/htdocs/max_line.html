Draw maximum line

Maximum values for periods of time specified in "Avg/Max" field in the form are computed from the original data samples normally received in one second intervals. For long-term graphs, the maximum values can be much higher than average values and can reduce clarity of the graph. For example, the first graph below shows one week of monitoring including the maximum values, whereas the second graph shows the same period of monitoring without the maximum values.

You can choose one of the following options:

  • Yes - the maximum values will be shown in all generated graphs
  • No - the maximum values will not be shown in all generated graphs
  • Auto - the maximum values will be shown only in graphs covering time span of 30 minutes or less
